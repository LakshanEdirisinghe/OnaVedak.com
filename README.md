# OnaVedak.com

A global online marketplace that connects customers with trusted skilled workers and service professionals.

## Overview

OnaVedak.com is a platform designed to help customers quickly find and hire skilled workers for home, business, and maintenance services. The system allows service providers to create professional profiles, showcase their skills, and receive job opportunities from customers in their area.

The platform acts as a bridge between customers and professionals, making it easier to find reliable services whenever needed.

## Target Service Categories

The platform supports a wide range of skilled workers, including:

- Electricians
- Plumbers
- Car Mechanics
- Gardeners
- Cleaners
- Carpenters
- Masons
- Painters
- Air Conditioning Technicians
- Handymen
- Construction Workers
- Home Maintenance Specialists

## Problem Statement

Many customers struggle to find reliable and trusted service providers during emergencies or for planned maintenance work. At the same time, many skilled workers have difficulty finding new customers and job opportunities.

OnaVedak.com solves this problem by providing a centralized platform where both parties can easily connect.

## How It Works

### For Customers

1. Create an account.
2. Search for a required service.
3. Browse available professionals.
4. View ratings, reviews, and profiles.
5. Hire a professional.
6. Complete the service.
7. Leave a review.

### For Service Providers

1. Register on the platform.
2. Create a professional profile.
3. Add skills and service categories.
4. Receive job requests.
5. Accept jobs.
6. Complete the work.
7. Receive ratings and reviews.
